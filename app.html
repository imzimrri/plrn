Applicant

Applicant Inquiry

There are many reasons you may not have access to traditional financing. The Partner Lending Referral Network provides small businesses with access to capital so they can grow, create jobs and strengthen their communities. We are not a bank or an online lender. We are a network of economic development agencies with a mission to foster a thriving business community. Partnerships and collaboration are the core of our work.

The Partner Lending Referral Network is a program where lenders, economic development agencies and community partners collaborate to support small business owners and help you succeed.

Who Can Apply

If you have recently been declined for a commercial loan, and your business is in Butte, Glenn, or Tehama Counties in northern California, apply today.

If you are outside of the above service area, you must be referred by a partner. Just give your lender, Economic Development agency or Community Partner this website link so they can become a partner in the network.

Applicants must be at least 18-years of age, reside, and conduct business in the USA.

Application for Business Owners in Butte, Glenn, and Tehama Counties, CA Only
