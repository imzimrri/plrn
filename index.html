Small Business Loan Solutions

Welcome to the Partner Lending Referral Network

The Partner Lending Referral Network is a collaboration between lenders, economic development agencies, and community professionals who provide access to capital for California based small businesses that may not qualify for traditional business loan offerings.

We believe these businesses are critical to the growth and well being of our communities. The Partner Lending Referral Network connects unmet business financing needs with resources who can fill the gap.

Join the network and begin referring clients to a trusted partner for ethical lending solutions.
